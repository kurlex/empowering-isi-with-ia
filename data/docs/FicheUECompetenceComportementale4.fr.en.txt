ECUE Personal development and Socializing fact sheet
 
Teaching Unit: Behavioral Skills IV
 
Constituent Element Plan (ECUE)
Prerequisites:
 
 The student benefits from an introduction to the first part of the TOEIC
 
 
Constituent Element Learning Objectives (CELU):
 
 
At the end of the component, the student will be able to:
Chapter 3: Internet and the marketing mix
Chapter 4: E-commerce  
Customer relations and digitalization of the point of sale  
Chapter 5: M-Marketing: Mobile Marketing
Chapter 6: Case Studies
The course is based on a pragmatic approach that allows the student to contribute
actively in defining concepts based on business case studies, studied at the beginning of
each chapter. The interest is to involve the student in the work of reflection and brainstorming and to
thus develop their interpretation skills in an approach to preparing for the resolution of
problems and the proposal of solutions from a group dynamics perspective, which figures
among the main missions of a company engineer.
 
 OE1: Acquire English communication skills
