2
 
The course is based on a pragmatic approach that allows the student to
actively contribute to the definition of concepts based on case studies of companies, studied
at the beginning of each chapter. The interest is to involve the student in the work of reflection and
brainstorming and thus develop their interpretation skills in an approach of
preparation for problem solving and proposing solutions from a perspective of
group dynamics, which is one of the main missions of a company engineer.
