Description of the content of the constituent element
This PROJECT will take place in the form of a workshop to synthesize the different concepts related to IoT
(IoT networks, Cloud, Big Data, embedded objects, machine learning).  
The student will implement a complete IoT architecture: sensors-gateway-server-applications.
