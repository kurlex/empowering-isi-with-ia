Teaching Unit: Connected Objects
ECUE no 1.1: Home automation and connected buildings
Constituent Element Plan (ECUE)
Prerequisites:
 
1- Automation
2- Power electronics
3- Local networks  
4- Communication bus
 
 
Constituent Element Learning Objectives (CELU):
 
At the end of the component, the student will be able to:
OE1: Define the concept of intelligent, connected, autonomous and ecological building
OE2: Present the different technologies of home automation and technical management of
wired, wireless, proprietary and standard buildings.
OE3: design a building command and control network.
OE4: choose the equipment needed to successfully complete the creation of such a network
OE5: Compare existing technologies, their advantages and weaknesses.
